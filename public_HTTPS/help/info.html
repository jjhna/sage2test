SAGE2 Information

Information

SAGE2 is rearchitected from the ground up based on SAGE (the Scalable Adaptive Graphics Environment). SAGE2 is implemented entirely in Javascript operating under the Chrome browser.

 

SAGE2 is funded by a National Science Foundation award to the Laboratory for Advanced Visualization and Applications at the University of Hawaiʻi at Mānoa, with a subaward to the Electronic Visualization Laboratory at the University of Illinois at Chicago.

 

SAGE2 and SAGE are trademarks of the University of Illinois Board of Trustees. Major funding for SAGE2 and SAGE is provided by National Science Foundation award ACI-1339772.
